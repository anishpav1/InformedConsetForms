SKIN WARMING

 MRI Radiofrequency has the potential to cause tissue heating. The Technologist will take several precautions
to avoid this. Alert the technologist immediately if you notice any heating sensations during your MRI scan.
PIERCINGS, COSMETIC IMPLANTS, TATTOOS AND PERMANENT MAKEUP

 A small number of patients have experienced transient skin irritation, swelling, bruising or heating sensations at the
site of piercings, cosmetic implants, tattoos and permanent makeup in association with MR procedures.
Individuals with these items should inform the technologist so precautions can be taken.
