• This is a consent addendum for research participation.  It contains important additional information about this study and what to expect if you participate (or continue to participate). • Your participation is voluntary.  You may refuse to participate (or to continue to participate) in this study.  If you decide to take part in the study, you may leave the study at any time.  No matter what decision you make, there will be no penalty to you and you will not lose any of your usual benefits. If you are a student or employee at The University of Arizona, your decision will not affect your grades or employment status.
